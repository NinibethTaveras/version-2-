Tips On How To Deal With Anxiety
    Take time out for yourself

Do whatever you enjoy

    Eat a good meal

DO NOT SKIP MEALS, keep a healthy snack with you at all times

    Get a good night sleep every night

This is pretty straight forwards, sleep as much hours as you can

    Avoid alcohol and caffeine beverages

Alcohol and caffine can trigger panic attacks

    Take Deep Breathes

Count up to 10 very slowly, if not count to 20 slowlyy as well.
